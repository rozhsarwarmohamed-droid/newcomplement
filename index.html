📖 Word Glossary

🧩

Complement

The number you need to add to reach a total. Like puzzle pieces — one piece + its complement = the whole puzzle!

35 + 65 = 100  →  65 is the complement of 35

💭

Estimate

A close guess — not the exact answer, but a good one! Round numbers to make it easier to calculate in your head.

980 − 260  ≈  1000 − 300 = 700

✖️

Multiple

Numbers in a times-table. Multiples of 10 are: 10, 20, 30, 40, 50… They always end in zero!

10 × 1 = 10  ·  10 × 5 = 50  ·  10 × 54 = 540

📈

Scale

To make all numbers bigger or smaller by the same amount. If you scale 35 + 65 = 100 by ×10, you get 350 + 650 = 1000!

×10 means multiply each number by 10

🔢

Round

To change a number to the nearest 10 or 100. Look at the last digit — if it's 5 or more, round UP! If it's 4 or less, round DOWN.

980 rounds to 1000  ·  260 rounds to 300
